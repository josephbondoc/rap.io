Nujabes

Jun Seba (February 7, 1974 â€“ February 26, 2010) was a Japanese hip hop producer and DJ who recorded under the name Nujabes, the reverse spelling of his name in Japanese order. Nujabes mastered sampling to create music, digging frequently in latin music. Nujabes was also owner of the Shibuya record stores, T Records, Guinness Records, and founder of the independent label Hydeout Productions. He died in a car accident on February 26, 2010. Even with a short career, Nujabes' legacy still lives on, with tributes albums by labelmates to a dedicated subreddit.
