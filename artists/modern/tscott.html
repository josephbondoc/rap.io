Travis Scott

Jacques Berman Webster II (born April 30, 1992), better known as Travis Scott (previously stylized as “Travi$ Scott”) is a rapper and producer from Missouri City, Texas, located within the Houston Metropolitan area. Scott began producing and releasing beats via MySpace at 16. He moved to Los Angeles after dropping out of The University of Texas at San Antonio following his sophomore year, adopting the name Travi$ Scott in homage to his uncle named Travis, and his musical idol Kid Cudi (real name Scott Miscudi).
