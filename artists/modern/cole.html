J. Cole

Jermaine Lamarr Cole, better known as J. Cole, is a rapper and producer who was born in Frankfurt, Germany (January 28th, 1985) and raised in Fayetteville, North Carolina. He gained a passion for rap at a young age and first went by the names Blaza and Therapist (the latter was given to him by Bomb Sheltah, a respected rap group from Fayetteville) before settling on his own name.
